1️⃣ **Demand ML Foundation:** Built a robust time-series model (Prophet) that natively learns from historical shocks, yielding a highly accurate baseline.
2️⃣ **The Physics of Inventory:** Integrated standardized logic to map that forecast against constraints (Lead Times, Holding Costs) to calculate Reorder Points and EOQ.
3️⃣ **The Executive UI:** Deployed a Streamlit Web-App that serves as a "What-If" simulator. Planners can inject hypothetical scenarios (like +30% demand) and instantly watch the system rewrite PO requirements.

**The biggest insight?**
Pushing a target Service Level from 95% to 99% completely blows up your required Safety Stock. Visualizing this trade-off saves companies millions.
